Who is Sketch City?

We are an open, non-profit community of technology advocates and civic hackers:

meetup = hacks, in-person events, real connections
github = share code, data, documentation
ideas = browse or submit project ideas
slack = research, organize, chat
facebook = announcements, events, and discussions
contact = reach out to the organizers

Sample projects

Browse new project ideas or submit your own in the project ideas repo.

Kuukibot is a twitterbot that tweets air quality warnings.
John’s Beware is a study of sex demand and human trafficking.
The District Finder API helps you retrieve all government entities for an address.
We’re also working on a neighborhood broadband survey, a participatory budgeting pilot program, and several other ongoing projects. Join slack to learn more!
